Vegetarian pasta

Italian cuisine is always very romantic. You will surprise your mate with this unique and easy recipe. Suitable for vegetarians and spaghetti lovers. This will be one of the best food you ever try.

You need pasta, mushrooms and tomato sauce. Boil the paste for 10 minutes and add the remaining products. The fragrance will enchant your guests. The recipe is suitable for romantic dinners.
