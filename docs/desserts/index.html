  5. 200 g of chocolate
  6. 6 tablespoons cocoa
  7. 2 tablespoons rum
  8. 100 g jam of orange peel

Receipt:

  • In a deep bowl, oil, sugar and egg are poured with a mixer.
  • Add pre-mixed and sieved flour, cocoa and baking powder. Everything is crushed with a mixer until a cake dough is obtained, and finally the rum is poured into it.
  • The mixture is poured into a greased and sprinkled with a flour rectangular tray. The pastry is baked for 50 minutes at 200 degrees. Once done, it is watered with the remaining chocolate melted with some butter or fresh milk.

  4. 1 glass of water
  5. 2 tablespoons jam jam
  6. 1 tablespoon butter
  7. 1 baculber
  8. 1 salt

Receipt:

  • From fresh milk, eggs, flour, melted butter and salt, pancake dough with a slightly less consistency is broken.
  • From the dough, pancakes are baked until the amount is exhausted. Of these products, about 10-12 pancakes should be obtained.
  • Ready pancakes are smeared with strawberry jam. Always be careful when preparing pancakes, a few seconds inadvertently and they can burn.

Home Ice cream

  • Preparation

    12 min

  • Cooking

    43 min

  • Portions

    3-6
5.9(234)

1741 views

Products:

  1. 1/2 liter full-fat fresh milk
  2. 3 pieces eggs
  3. 100 g sugar
  4. 2 sachets vanilla
  5. 200 g of berries
  6. 1 cup of orange juice
  7. 100 g walnuts grated

Receipt:

  • Put the milk to boil. Beat the eggs with sugar and add 2 vanilla. Add the egg mixture to the milk and stir the mixture until it boils and gently thickens. Allow to cool aside, occasionally stirring.
  • Remove the finished mixture into a bowl and blow with a blender for a while to get air into the mixture.
  • Put ice cream in the camera to freeze. Multiply the mixture with a mixer in order not to form large crystals, but to become homogeneous. Serve the milk ice cream with jam, fruit or topping at your choice.
