Crudo

  • Preparation

    43 min

  • Cooking

    65 min

  • Portions

    1-4
5.1(344)

3531 views

Products:

  1. 320 g yellow cheese
  2. 1 for pizza, ready dough
  3. 100g mozzarella
  4. 150 g "pepperoni" salami
  5. 50 g olives
  6. 50 ml. tomato sauce

Receipt:

  • From the finished dough, we plow off a pizza swamp with a size to our taste.
  • Cut the salami, olives and mozzarella. Place the tomato sauce, olives and mozzarella on the dough.
  • Add the grated cheese and top the chopped salami.
  • Bake in a preheated oven at 180 degrees for about 15-18 minutes.

Caprese

  • Preparation

    54 min

  • Cooking

    21 min

  • Portions

    4-6
5.6(342)

5644 views

Products:

  1. 100 - 150 g of slices bacon
  2. 4 - 5 pcs. marinated mushrooms
  3. 3 - 4 pcs. olives without stones
  4. 200 g yellow cheese
  5. 4 - 5 tablespoons tomato paste
  6. 1 tablespoons savory
  7. 2 tablespoons pepper

Receipt:

  • The quick-breaking dough is divided into 2 parts (for two small pizzas) and spread in spreaded trays.
  • Garnish with tomato paste, diluted with 1-2 tablespoons water, shredded cheese, put bacon, cut sterilized mushrooms of thin, yellow cheese, olives, savory and black pepper.
  • Spread a teaspoon of oil on each pizza. Bake in a heated oven at 240 degrees for 15-20 minutes.
