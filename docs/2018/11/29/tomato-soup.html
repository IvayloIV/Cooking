Tomato soup

This soup is suitable for hot summer days, so it can be consumed cold. The variety of tomatoes in the recipe can be replaced by your choice.

Brush and cut well ripe tomatoes, put them in hot water. Add spices like parsley and red pepper. Suitable for hot and cold consumption.
